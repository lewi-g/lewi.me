Hi, I'm Lewi Gilamichael, NYC based web developer with a fondness for using Node, React, Redux and GraphQL. When I am not eating, sleeping and breathing code, I spend my time swing dancing and teaching swing dances. I also spend a fair amount of time reading about and examining the forces that affect individual, community and social change.

In my former life, I spent 7 years as a Clinical Laboratory Scientist in Microbiology at Johns Hopkins Hospital. The daily pattern of managing and streamlining multiple concurrent processes to deliver accurate results timely is baked into my own Operating System. Whether it be cooking shrimp and grits for a community brunch for the first time or making a responsive website, Testing and Quality Assurance are values I endeavor to maintain in all of my projects.
