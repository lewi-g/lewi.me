Hi, I'm Lewi Gilamichael, NYC-based web developer with a fondness for using Node, React, Redux and GraphQL. When I am not eating, sleeping and breathing code, I spend my time swing dancing and teaching swing dances. If there's time left, I like to read about the forces that affect individual, community and social change.

7 years spent as a Clinical Laboratory Scientist in Microbiology at Johns Hopkins Hospital has left me with a taste for excellence and accuracy. The daily pattern of managing and streamlining multiple concurrent processes to deliver accurate results in a timely manner is baked into my own Operating System. Whether it be my first time cooking shrimp and grits for my house's brunch or making a responsive website, testing and quality assurance are values I endeavor to maintain in all of my projects.
